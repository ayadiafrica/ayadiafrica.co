About AYADI

African Youth Awakening for Development Initiative (AYADI) was founded in 2016 and is a legally registered Community-Based Organization (CBO) operating in Western Rwanda.

From its base in Nyamasheke District, AYADI has expanded its outreach to Rusizi, Karongi, and Nyabihu, empowering rural communities through sustainable development programs.

Our Mission

To empower rural women and youth through environmental protection, entrepreneurship, climate action, and practical skills development.

Our Vision

A self-reliant and environmentally responsible society where women and youth actively contribute to sustainable economic growth.

Our Impact Since 2016

  • Over 1,200 women trained in entrepreneurship and practical skills
  • More than 520 youth empowered through vocational programs
  • Teenage mothers supported with income-generating skills
  • Community savings groups (VSLA) strengthened
  • Youth supported to return to school
  • Tree nursery and reforestation initiative in preparation
